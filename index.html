Welcome to my Home Page

My name is Josh, and this is my college final but this will eventually be my personal website for mostly all of my music projects. But I also mess with computers, electronics, and anything I find interesting in the moment.

The Purpose of the Site

This basically doubles as an about section but in its current state here and the website as a whole is a skeleton for whatever music content I create. A lot of choices were made for that purpose and that purpose alone. I shoehorned in some content, had some fun, and gave it some purpose beyond "just a grade". The purpose was more so for learning and connecting with the topic beyond surface level, though a lot of what I learned never got used I am still glad I learned them. If you're one of my classmates reading this. I do want to state for the record: creating your website for a grade is far from a bad thing and what I am doing is risky and stupid. If you're Professor Harkness, I apologize for the confusion. Normally your supposed to design it like some job resume or something more grounded.

I don't really mind taking a small hit to the grade if it means that I had some fun learning a topic I don't normally like interacting with and despite everything. After all learning should be fun and I had quite a bit of fun creating this, though I do wish I had more time to finish at least one song to display on this site and am a little displeased about shoehorning in a (lackluster) practice recording and a placeholder sfx for the music page. Though I did get to use the best collaborator ever for the Collaborations page, however unhuman they were.

To anyone reading this, Be it professor or classmate. I hope you can appreciate the audacity of such an unconventional route for a college final even if a bit annoying. But I promise, I mean no malice out of it. I just wanted to enjoy the process and learn, in which I sure did.

For site content you can click on the nav links above.
