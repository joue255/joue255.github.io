Improvised Organ Practice

I recommend lowering the volume before listening to this.

This was a practice recording and was done on my phone hence the poor quality and the background noise. I think I was a month in learning the instrument back in September. The focus was improvising based on feel, I used my Hammond Organ and drove the organ's audio signal to a Fender Champion 20 Guitar Modeling Amp. I cranked the gain to push into overdrive and incorporated heavy use of Wah FX and dynamically played by adjusting drawbar settings, adjusting eq and a load of other settings on both the organ and the amp which I had perched on top of the organ. The result was an eerie almost cinematic quality of playing, it was weird sounding and has rough spots but it grew on me and I liked it enough so I kept the entire video. Though you guys only get the audio.

More likely I will explore this specific distorted organ sound in the future as under better recording circumstances it can sound really good.
