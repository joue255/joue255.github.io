Improvised Organ Practice

I recommend lowering the volume before listening to this.

This was a practice recording and was done on my phone hence the poor quality and the background noise. I think I was a month in learning the instrument back in September. The focus was improvising based on feel, I used my Hammond Organ and drove the organ's audio signal to a Fender Champion 20 Guitar Modeling Amp. I cranked the gain to push into overdrive and incorporated heavy use of Wah FX and dynamically played by adjusting drawbar settings, adjusting eq and a load of other settings on both the organ and the amp which I had perched on top of the organ. The result was an eerie almost cinematic quality of playing, it was weird and has rough spots but I liked it enough to keep it.
